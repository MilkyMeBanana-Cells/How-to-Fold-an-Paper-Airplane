Paper Airplanes

Making paper airplanes is a fun and easy hobby anyone can enjoy. Whether you're folding for fun or flying for distance, there's a design for everyone. Below are five helpful links to get started!

  • Fold 'N Fly

    A website full of paper airplane designs, from beginner to expert.

  • Wikipedia: Paper Plane

    Learn about the history and science behind paper airplanes.

  • Scientific American

    This article explains how paper airplanes stay in the air.

  • YouTube: Easy Paper Plane

    A simple, step-by-step tutorial to follow along with.

  • Instructables: Paper Airplanes

    A collection of creative designs and instructions from users.
